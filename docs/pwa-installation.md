# PWA Installation Guide

Stellar-Spend is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works seamlessly on mobile and desktop devices. Follow these instructions to install and use the app offline.

## Installation

### On Mobile (iOS & Android)

#### Android
1. Open Stellar-Spend in Chrome or any Chromium-based browser
2. Tap the menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Select **"Install app"** or **"Add to Home screen"**
4. Confirm the installation
5. The app will appear on your home screen

#### iOS (Safari)
1. Open Stellar-Spend in Safari
2. Tap the Share button (↗️) at the bottom
3. Scroll down and select **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Enter a name for the app (or keep "Stellar-Spend")
5. Tap **"Add"**
6. The app will appear on your home screen

### On Desktop

#### Windows / Linux (Chrome, Edge, Brave)
1. Open Stellar-Spend in your browser
2. Click the install icon (⬇️) in the address bar
3. Click **"Install"** in the popup
4. The app will open in a standalone window

#### macOS (Chrome, Edge, Brave)
1. Open Stellar-Spend in your browser
2. Click the menu icon (⋮) in the top-right corner
3. Select **"Install app"**
4. The app will open in a standalone window

## Features

### Offline Support
- View your transaction history even without internet
- Access cached pages and data
- Automatic sync when connection is restored

### Background Sync
- Failed transactions are automatically retried when you're back online
- No manual intervention needed
- Transactions are stored securely on your device

### Push Notifications
- Get notified when transactions complete
- Receive alerts for failed transactions
- Enable notifications in your browser settings

### Shortcuts
- Quick access to start an offramp
- Direct link to transaction history
- Available from your home screen or app menu

## Offline Functionality

When offline, you can:
- ✅ View your transaction history
- ✅ Review past offramp details
- ✅ Prepare bank account information
- ❌ Cannot initiate new transactions (requires internet)
- ❌ Cannot fetch live FX rates

## Troubleshooting

### App Won't Install
- Ensure you're using a supported browser (Chrome, Edge, Safari, Brave)
- Check that you have a stable internet connection
- Clear your browser cache and try again

### Notifications Not Working
1. Check browser notification permissions
2. Go to Settings → Notifications
3. Ensure Stellar-Spend has permission to send notifications
4. On mobile, check your device notification settings

### Offline Page Shows
- This means you're offline or the server is unreachable
- Check your internet connection
- The app will automatically reconnect when available

### Background Sync Not Working
- Ensure your browser supports Background Sync (Chrome, Edge, Brave)
- Keep the app installed on your device
- Don't force-close the app on mobile

## Performance Tips

1. **Install on Home Screen** - Faster launch and better integration
2. **Enable Notifications** - Stay updated on transaction status
3. **Keep App Updated** - Browser automatically updates the app
4. **Clear Cache Periodically** - Helps maintain performance

## Privacy & Security

- All data is stored locally on your device
- No data is sent to third-party servers except for transactions
- Your private keys are never stored in the app
- Transactions are signed in your wallet, not on our servers
